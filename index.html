Math-smash

Instructions

  • You'll be asked very simple maths quetions but you'll be given only 2 seconds of time to answer each

  • You'll get 50 points for each correct response and -50 for every wrong

  • The question will keep on changing every 2 seconds so your answer should be correct and accurate

  • Nobody was able to cross more than 6000

  • You have thunder accuracy if you cross more than even 4000 points

  • You have to get as many points as you can in 100 seconds

  • Play it with Desktop/Laptop for better functionality






Note : Do not write on the input box, just type the numbers and it will enter it itself and press "Enter" to submit
